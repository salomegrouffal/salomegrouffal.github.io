My research topics

Ph.D topic: Characterisation of transiting exoplanets in the habitable zone

Exoplanet detection

I am working on exoplanet detection and characterisation. I mainly use the methods of transits, radial velocities, Transit-Timing-Variations, and Rossiter-McLaughlin effect.

The amazing planetary system around HIP41378

HIP41378 is a fascinating planetary system hosting five transiting planets. It is a complex system with long-period planets that we use as a testbed for the PLATO mission.

The SOPHIE spectrograph

I worked on correcting radial velocity systematics on the SOPHIE spectrograph at OHP using Gaussian Processes and temperature and pressure variations of the spectrograph.

The PLATO mission

The PLATO mission is planned to be launched in 2026. I am working on the preparation of the mission with long-period planet detection.
